How I operate

A leader is measured on judgment

Not interfaces — the judgment behind them, and the team that can repeat it. Three things I'm accountable for.

01

Empathy at scale

I design for the users everyone else forgets — Tier 2/3 users, time-poor doctors, compliance teams. The hard edges of the audience are where the real product lives.

02

Clarity from ambiguity

My job is to rally product, engineering, and domain experts around one user-centered direction while the problem is still undefined. Alignment is a design deliverable.

03

Outcomes over aesthetics

Every project ships a number: fewer no-shows, better risk visibility, more inclusive access to finance. Pretty is table stakes. Impact is the point.
